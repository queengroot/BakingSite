Pistachio Pudding

This is a great recipe that's quick, delicious, and refreshing, especially in the summer. It takes about 5 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • Pistachio pudding (either 2 small packages or 1 big one.)
  • 1 can pineapple tidbits or chunks
  • Tropical marshmallows
  • Milk for the pudding (usually around 4 cups)

Directions

  1. Follow the directions and make the pudding.
  2. Add as many marshmallows and pineapple as you want. Personally, I like more marshmallows than pineapple.
