Breakfast Burrito

This is a great recipe for mornings. When you make them, make a big batch because you can freeze them and then pop them in the microwave for 45 seconds to a minute before going to school or work.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of any cheese you like
  • 1 lb sausage or bacon
  • 1 bag hashbrowns
  • Picante (optional)
  • 1 green pepper

Directions

  1. Make the scrambled eggs. Mix six eggs, a dash of milk, one diced green pepper, how ever much cheese you want and salt and pepper. Put in a hot pan and cook until done.
  2. Cook the bacon or sausage.
  3. Cook the hashbrowns in butter and oil. Add salt and pepper, and if you have some, garlic powder tastes great in it.
  4. Burritos, assemble! Layer the eggs, bacon/sausage, and hashbrowns in a tortilla. If desired, add picante sauce.
