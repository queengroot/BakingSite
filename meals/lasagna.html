Mom's Lasagna

This is my favorite recipe of my mom's. She's been making it for as long as I can remember, and it is so delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb oven-ready lasagna
  • 2 jars of marinara
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 24 oz cottage cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan
  • 1 teaspooon dried parsley
  • 8 cups mozzarella

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Dice the onion. In medium frypan, brown the 1lb ground beef with the onion.
  3. In medium bowl, mix together the ricotta, cottage cheese, parmesan, eggs, and parsley.
  4. Layer noodles, marinara, cheese mix, meat, mozzarella. Save two cups of mozzarella to put on top.
  5. Cover with tin foil and put in the oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
  6. Uncover and add the remaining mozzarella cheese. Put back in oven until cheese melts.
  7. Let sit for about a hour. Serve with garlic bread and a salad for a wonderful meal.
